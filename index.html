ConnectTel customers Churn Analysis and Prediction Leveraging Machine Learning

Connectel was faced with a very high rate of customers attrition which required urgent and effective strategy to tackle it.
Through a rigorous Exloratory Data Analysis using Python, likely causes of this unhealthy exodus of the customers were unearthed with the aid of many highly revealing visualizations. This led to valuable recommendations
With the proper application of supervised Machine Learning, using historical Data of customers' behaviour from the company database, predictions of most customers at the risk of churning were were made with high level of precision, which afford the Telecom giant the opportunity to proactively engage them and addres their common and peculiar greviances, Introduce more incentives and loyalty programmes. This led to 19% reduction in the churning rate
